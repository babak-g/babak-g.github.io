Lahjay — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Lahjay (the “App”) is a personal Persian‑language learning application operated by Lahjay Company (“we,” “us,” or “our”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Lahjay, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide – When you create or import flashcards or contact us, you may provide:

  • Content you add to the App (e.g., text or images on flashcards).
  • Optional account or contact details (such as email) if you choose to sync decks or request support.

Automatically collected information – Through Apple‑provided analytics and diagnostics (if you opt in), we receive anonymized, aggregated data about crashes, feature usage, and device information (e.g., model, iOS version). This data cannot identify you personally.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Lahjay’s features, including the Leitner review system, token management, and local progress tracking.
  • Respond to support inquiries or feedback you send us.
  • Monitor and analyze App performance, crashes, and usage trends to improve stability and usability.

4. Third‑Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share information only:

  • With service providers who process data on our behalf in compliance with this Policy (e.g., Apple iCloud if you enable iCloud backup).
  • When required by law, subpoena, or other legal process.

5. Data Retention

Your flashcard decks and progress remain on your device and in your private iCloud storage (if enabled) until you delete them or uninstall the App. Crash logs and analytics retained by Apple follow Apple’s standard retention schedules.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including iOS sandboxing and Apple‑managed encryption for any iCloud data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children’s Privacy

Lahjay is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted within the App or at our website. Material changes will be announced in‑App. Please review periodically.
