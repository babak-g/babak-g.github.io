Lahjay

Lahjay is a self-guided Persian-language learning app built around a smart Leitner flash-card system, offline vocab packs, and bite-sized grammar hints.

  • Adaptive spaced-repetition decks (Beginner → Advanced)
  • Audio clips by native speakers
  • Token system to unlock challenges
  • 100% offline — no account required
