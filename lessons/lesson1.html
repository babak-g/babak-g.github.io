Introduction to Persian Script

Welcome to your first lesson in Persian! The Persian alphabet (ÿßŸÑŸÅÿ®ÿß€å ŸÅÿßÿ±ÿ≥€å) is one of the most beautiful writing systems in the world. It consists of 32 letters and is written from right to left, just like Arabic and Hebrew.

Each letter can have up to four different forms depending on its position in a word:

  • Isolated: When the letter stands alone
  • Initial: At the beginning of a word
  • Medial: In the middle of a word
  • Final: At the end of a word

Don't worry about memorizing all forms at once - we'll start with the basic shapes and pronunciations!
