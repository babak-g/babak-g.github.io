Lesson 2: Colors and Fruits

Discover the vibrant world of Persian colors and delicious fruits

Building Your Vocabulary

Now that you know the Persian alphabet, let's expand your vocabulary with everyday words! Colors and fruits are fundamental vocabulary that you'll use constantly in Persian conversation.

In this lesson, you'll learn how to identify and pronounce common colors and fruits in Persian. Notice how the letters from Lesson 1 combine to create these beautiful words!

🍎 Fruits - میوه‌ها (Mive-ha)

Explore these delicious fruits and their Persian names. Many of these you practiced writing in Lesson 1!

🍎
سیب
Apple
seeb
A crisp, sweet fruit that's perfect for snacking. Often red, green, or yellow in color.
🍌
موز
Banana
mooz
A tropical fruit with soft, sweet flesh. Yellow when ripe and packed with potassium.
🍊
پرتقال
Orange
porteghal
A citrus fruit rich in vitamin C. Named after the country Portugal in Persian.
🍇
انگور
Grapes
angoor
Small, round fruits that grow in clusters. Can be green, red, or purple.
🍓
توت فرنگی
Strawberry
toot farangi
Literally means "French berry" in Persian. Sweet, red, and heart-shaped.
🍑
گیلاس
Cherry
gilas
Small, round stone fruits that are typically red or dark red when ripe.
🍉
هندوانه
Watermelon
hendoovane
A large, refreshing fruit perfect for hot summer days. Green outside, red inside.
🥭
انبه
Mango
anbe
A tropical fruit with sweet, creamy flesh. Yellow-orange when ripe.
