Quick Start

  • Create a deck: Tap + Deck on the Home screen.
  • Add cards: Open the deck → + Card.
  • Study: Press Review; answer cards and tap Show Answer.
  • Sync iCloud: Enable in Settings to back up decks across devices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I only have three tokens?
Tokens encourage focused sessions; you earn a new one each day or by completing review streaks.

Can I import Anki decks?
Yes. In Home, tap Import and select your .apkg file.

Does Lahjay work offline?
Absolutely — all content is stored on your device.

How do I reset progress?
Long-press a deck in Home → Reset Progress.
