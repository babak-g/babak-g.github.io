Getting Started

Step 1: Sign up for beta access on our homepage by entering your email address. You'll receive an invitation when the app launches.
Step 2: Download the Lahjay app from the App Store. The app will be available for only iOS devices at the moment.
Step 4: Start your first lesson! Our interactive approach celebrates your unique accent while helping you connect with Persian culture and language.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior knowledge of Persian/Farsi?
Not at all! Lahjay is designed for learners of all levels, from complete beginners to those looking to reconnect with their heritage. Our adaptive learning system adjusts to your current level and learning pace.
How much will Lahjay cost?
We're still finalizing our pricing structure, but we're committed to making Persian language learning accessible. We'll likely offer both free content and premium features. Beta testers will receive special early access pricing.
Can I use Lahjay offline?
We're working on offline functionality for core lessons and exercises, so you can continue learning even without an internet connection. This feature will be available in our initial release.
