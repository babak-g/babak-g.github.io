## Introduction

I'd love to meet any person who is a bigger Nick's fan than me. I'll admit there were times in my life awhile ago where I would devour 3 of these pints within an hour [I'd highly reccommend reading my 'Health Journey' blog post if you're interested why], but tldr: gg stomach.

I don't think I can consume more than 1 pint nowadays, but I will say, I truly LOVE every part of my day where I get to eat my pint of Nick's ice cream.  I maximize foods that make me feel-good and those that I think taste good to me. Nick's is one of those, where even though it has CRAZY fiber and some things in the ingredients list that I cannot pronounce, I love that I can eat a whole pint in peace and really enjoy something of that volume that tastes like ice cream. I don't feel bad after eating it (I will say I don't feel like rainbows), but it makes me happy. I'd say more mentally happy than bodily, which I love.

## Why I decided to make this list

I was talking to a good coworker of mine and we started bonding over protein snacks. He then mentioned, him and his partner keep a list of ice cream reviews that they love. I was shocked. I've actually been wanting to write down some products that I really like and my personal rankings. Nick's was one of them. I was so ready to hear his top ice cream list being Nick's but he said Halo Top. I was shocked, yet again. Not that there's anything wrong with Halo Top, but I just love Nick's so much, I don't know why it evoked a feeling out of me.

## Babak != Comparisons

One of the books I read recently mentioned that comparisons are judgement. The more I read this book, the more I wanted to stop comparing things in my life. I didn't want to evaluate something based off something else, and that I want to truly value something based off how it makes me feel. The idea of ranking lists are kind of comparisons, but I felt like this was okay because I see this more as:

"When Babak walks into a grocery stores for his daily Nick's ice cream, which one is he going to pick up?"

That's how I will present this ranking and it is truly my opinion of what I like when I want Nick's. Take that as you will.

## Few Disclaimers

1) I very much enjoy bits/pieces of anything in my ice cream. Some crunch always hits a little harder for me.
2) I like Nick's chocolate-based flavors more than vanilla. I'm a vanilla person, don't get me wrong, but Nick's vanilla just doesn't do it for me much.
3) For non-Nick's ice cream, Cookies & Cream & Birthday Cake are top flavors usually for me, and I am very biased toward nutty flavors (i.e. peanut).
4) If all these flavors were available everyday to me, I wouldn't necessarily start top-down. Some are very dependent on my mood, which I will say I fluctuate between my top 8. But my frequeuncy of getting for example: Triple Brownie is much higher than Swedish Munchies.
