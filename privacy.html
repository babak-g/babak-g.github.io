Protecting your privacy while celebrating your journey

1 Introduction

Lahjay ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data. By using the Lahjay Persian language learning app and services, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

2 Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Your email address for beta access
  • Lesson progress and learning achievements
  • Optional profile information
  • Any content you create or share within the app

Automatically collected information:

  • App performance data
  • Crash reports (with your consent)
  • General learning progress statistics to improve our teaching methods

3 How We Use Information

  • Send you beta access notifications and important app updates
  • Respond to support requests and technical issues
  • Analyze app usage to enhance features and user experience
  • Celebrate linguistic diversity and cultural connections through our platform

4 Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data only with:

  • Trusted service providers who help us operate the app (such as cloud storage providers)
  • Analytics services that help us improve Lahjay
  • When legally required by applicable laws or regulations

5 Data Retention

Your learning progress and app content remain on your device (and in your chosen cloud backup if enabled) until you delete them. Email addresses for beta access are retained until you unsubscribe. Analytics data follows our service providers' retention schedules and is anonymized.
